Privacy Policy

Guess the Song Creator — last updated April 2026

1. What data we collect

When you connect your TikTok account, we temporarily store:

  • Your TikTok access token (to publish videos on your behalf)
  • Your TikTok user ID (to identify your account)

No other personal data is collected, stored, or processed.

2. How we use your data

The access token is used solely to publish videos to your TikTok account when you explicitly trigger the upload. It is stored locally on the server running this application and is never transmitted to any third party.

3. Data retention

Access tokens are stored only as long as you remain connected. You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time, which permanently removes your token from our system.

4. Third-party services

This application uses the following external services:

  • TikTok API — for video publishing
  • Creatomate — for video rendering
  • ElevenLabs — for text-to-speech audio generation

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We do not control how they process data.

5. Your rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by disconnecting your TikTok account within the app or contacting the developer directly.

6. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact the developer directly.

This policy may be updated at any time.
